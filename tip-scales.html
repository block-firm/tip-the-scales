WHO WE ARE

Determined to achieve the best results and provide the highest level of legal expertise, Block LLP was formed with one goal in mind—justice for our clients.

Being on the wrong end of an accident or injury can turn someone’s life upside down. Not only is that person a victim of an injury, but the people responsible for paying monetary damages often have abundant resources to avoid paying victims what they deserve.

At Block LLP, these types of incidents are what drive our mission. We want to fight for people who are unable to stand up to large corporations and insurance companies. We work tenaciously to get our clients the compensation they need to move forward.
